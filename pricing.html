Billing & Payment Questions
How does payment work?

When you're ready to activate your paid plan after your free trial, we send you a secure PayFast payment link via WhatsApp. You pay once to confirm your first month, and PayFast handles all recurring billing automatically every month after that — you never need to remember to pay.

What is PayFast and is it safe?

PayFast is South Africa's most trusted online payment gateway, used by thousands of local businesses. It is fully PCI-DSS compliant — your card details are encrypted and secure. LaafWeb never sees or stores your banking information; it goes directly to PayFast.

💳 Credit / Debit Card 🏦 Instant EFT 📱 SnapScan 🔷 Masterpass 🏧 EFT
When am I billed each month?

You are billed on the same calendar date each month as your first payment. For example, if you pay on the 10th, PayFast debits your account automatically on the 10th every month. You will receive a payment confirmation email each time.

What happens if a payment fails?

PayFast will notify you by email and we will follow up on WhatsApp. You have 7 days to update your payment details before your website and listing are temporarily suspended. Once payment is resolved, everything is reactivated immediately — no data is lost.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Simply WhatsApp us on 063 669 1391 or email laafstylfamily@gmail.com and we cancel your subscription right away. Your site stays live until the end of the current billing period. No penalties, no lock-in.

Is the setup fee refundable?

The R1,200 setup fee is non-refundable once work has commenced. That's why we offer a free 14-day trial — so you can see the full service before any setup fee is charged, and know exactly what you're getting before you commit.
