Billing & Payment Questions
How does payment work?
We send you a secure PayFast payment link via WhatsApp. You pay once for the setup fee, then monthly billing runs automatically.
What is PayFast and is it safe?
PayFast is South Africa's most trusted payment gateway — fully PCI-DSS compliant. We never see or store your card details.
💳 Credit / Debit Card 🏦 Instant EFT 📱 SnapScan 🔷 Masterpass 🏧 EFT
When am I billed each month?
On the same calendar date as your first payment. PayFast handles it automatically and sends you a confirmation each month.
How do I cancel?
Just WhatsApp us on 063 669 1391 or email us. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing month. No penalties, ever.
Is the setup fee refundable?
The setup fee is non-refundable once work has started — which is why we offer a 7-day free trial first. You see the full service before any setup fee is charged.
What do I need to provide to get started?
Your business name, what you do, your location, contact number, and any photos or logo you have. Phone photos work fine. No logo yet? We can help with that too.
How does the annual billing saving work?
Pay for 10 months upfront, get 12 months of service. The toggle at the top of this page shows you the annual price for each plan. Setup fees are still paid separately and only once.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?
Yes, anytime. Just WhatsApp us and we'll sort the switch. Upgrading is instant — new features go live the same day. Downgrading takes effect from your next billing cycle.
Do you build websites for businesses outside Langebaan?
Absolutely. We work remotely and have clients across South Africa. Everything is done via WhatsApp and email — no in-person meetings needed.
