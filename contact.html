Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about LaafWeb and getting your business online.

QHow do I list my business?

WhatsApp us on 063 669 1391 with your business name, category, location, and a short description. We'll have your listing live within 24 hours.

QHow much does it cost?

Plans start at R200/month with a once-off R1,200 setup fee. A free 14-day trial is available — no credit card required. See our pricing page for full details.

QHow fast will my website go live?

We build and launch your website within 24 hours of receiving your business details. Send us the info on WhatsApp and we handle the rest.

QCan I update my business info?

Yes — just WhatsApp or email us any changes and we'll update your listing and website, usually within 1 business day.

QWhich areas do you cover?

We focus on the West Coast of the Western Cape — Saldanha, Langebaan, Vredenburg, Velddrif, Paternoster, St Helena Bay, and surrounding towns.

QCan I cancel anytime?

Yes — no lock-in contracts. Cancel at any time via WhatsApp or email. Your service runs until the end of the current billing period with no penalties.
