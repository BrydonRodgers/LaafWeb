Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about LaafWeb.

QHow do I list my business on LaafWeb?

WhatsApp us on 063 669 1391 to get started. We'll build your website and directory listing within 24 hours of receiving your details.

QWhat does a LaafWeb website cost?

Plans start from R200/month. A once-off R1,200 setup fee covers your domain, hosting, SSL, and business email. 14-day free trial available.

QCan I update my business information?

Yes — WhatsApp us any time with your updated details and we'll make changes same day. Growth and Pro plans include monthly or weekly updates.

QDo you only cover the West Coast?

For now we're focused on the West Coast — Saldanha, Vredenburg, Langebaan, Velddrif and surrounding towns. We're expanding soon.

QHow do payments work?

Monthly subscriptions are processed securely through PayFast. We never store your card details. Cancel any time with no penalties.

QWhat if I want to cancel?

Just WhatsApp us. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing month — no fees, no hassle. You can export your content before leaving.
