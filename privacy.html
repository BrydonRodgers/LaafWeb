1 Who We Are

LaafWeb is a service offered by LaafStyl (Pty) Ltd (Registration No: YOUR_REG_NUMBER), a company registered in the Republic of South Africa.

We provide website development, directory listing, lead tracking, and digital services to small businesses on the West Coast of the Western Cape. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

By using LaafWeb's website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

3 How We Use Your Data

We use your information only for legitimate business purposes:

  • To set up and manage your LaafWeb website and directory listing
  • To process and manage your monthly subscription via PayFast
  • To contact you regarding your account, service updates, or support via WhatsApp or email
  • To build and populate your business profile on the LaafWeb directory
  • To send service-related communications (not marketing, unless you opt in)
  • To comply with our legal obligations under South African law
⚠️

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4 Third-Party Services

To deliver our services, we work with the following trusted third-party providers:

  • PayFast — payment processing and subscription billing. View their privacy policy at payfast.co.za.
  • n8n — our automation platform that receives and processes onboarding form submissions securely.
  • GitHub Pages — static site hosting. GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type).

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law.

5 Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse, including HTTPS encryption on all LaafWeb pages and restricted access to personal data.

We retain your business and subscription data for as long as you are an active LaafWeb client, and for a reasonable period thereafter as required for accounting, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

If you request deletion of your data, we will remove your personal information from our active systems within 30 days, unless we are legally required to retain it.

6 Your Rights (POPIA)

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at laafstylfamily@gmail.com or WhatsApp us on 063 669 1391. We will respond within 30 days. You may also contact the Information Regulator at inforeg@justice.gov.za.

7 Cookies

PayFast may use cookies on their payment pages during checkout. Please refer to PayFast's cookie policy for details.
