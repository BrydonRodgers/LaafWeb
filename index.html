FAQ

Questions?
We've got answers.

The most common things people ask us before getting started.

Do I really not have to write anything? +
That's right. We ask you a few simple questions on WhatsApp — your business name, what you do, where you're based, and your phone number. We write all the content for you. You just review and approve. Most clients are surprised by how little effort it takes on their side.
What if I'm not happy with the website? +
We build first, you pay only if you love it. If something doesn't feel right, tell us and we'll fix it — no charge, no drama. You only pay when you're genuinely happy with the site. That's our promise.
Is domain and hosting really included? +
Yes, completely. Your monthly fee covers everything — domain name (e.g. yourbusiness.co.za), reliable SA hosting, SSL certificate (the padlock in the browser), and ongoing maintenance. No surprise renewal bills.
How long does it actually take? +
Most sites go live within 24 hours of us getting your info. Some are done in as little as a few hours. We don't outsource the work or leave it for tomorrow — we get it done same day wherever possible.
Can I cancel if I want to? +
Yes, absolutely. Give us 30 days' notice and we cancel your plan. No penalties, no difficult conversations. We want you to stay because you're happy with the service — not because you're locked in.
Do you build websites outside the West Coast? +
Absolutely. We're based in Saldanha Bay but we build for businesses all over South Africa — Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban, and everywhere in between. Everything is done via WhatsApp so location doesn't matter at all.
Will my site show up on Google? +
Yes. Every site we build has basic SEO built in — proper headings, meta descriptions, fast load speed, and mobile optimisation. We also submit your site to Google Search Console on your behalf. You'll start appearing in local searches within days.
What types of businesses do you work with? +
All kinds — plumbers, electricians, hair salons, beauty therapists, builders, contractors, guesthouses, B&Bs, restaurants, cleaning services, photographers, and much more. If you're a small business owner, we can help you.
