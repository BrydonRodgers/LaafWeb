FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to provide content for my website?
No — we write the copy for you based on a short WhatsApp conversation about your business. You just tell us what you do and we handle the rest. If you have photos, send them through. If not, we use high-quality stock images.
What if I want changes after launch?
On Launch and Business plans, changes come with your monthly support — just WhatsApp us and we'll update it, usually same day. On Buy Outright, you get one free revision at handover.
Do I own my website?
On monthly plans, we host and maintain the site — you own the brand and content but not the source files. If you cancel, we give you a 30-day export window. On Buy Outright, you get all source files and can host it anywhere — it's fully yours.
How long does it actually take?
For most sites we go live within 24 hours of receiving your business details and any photos. Complex sites with 4+ pages may take 2–3 days. We'll always give you a realistic timeline upfront.
Can I use my own domain name?
Yes — if you already have a domain we'll connect it. If you don't have one yet, we'll register one for you as part of your plan. We handle all the technical DNS setup.
What happens if I cancel?
No penalties, no lock-in. Cancel anytime via WhatsApp. Your site stays live until the end of your current billing period, then we take it offline. On Buy Outright, you own the files — nothing changes.
Do you only work with West Coast businesses?
We're based on the West Coast but we build websites for businesses across South Africa. We have a special focus on Langebaan, Saldanha, Vredenburg, Velddrif, and Malmesbury because we know those areas well.
